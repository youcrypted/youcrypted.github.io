Introduction.

youcryted.com pretends to be an encrypted communication platform to provide 100% of protection and confidentiality for user's data. Stands on the principle of peer-to-peer encrypted communication by combining AES-256 and XSalsa20 encryption algorithms.

For users, the 100% encrypted and secure social network. For apps, devices, machines, the 100% encrypted and secure communication platform.
